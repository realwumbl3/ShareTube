# ShareTube Commercial License

## Overview

ShareTube is available under a dual-license model:
- **Personal/Non-Commercial Use**: Free for personal projects
- **Commercial Use**: Requires a commercial license

## Commercial Licensing Options

### Startup License ($999/year)
- Up to $100K annual revenue
- 1 production deployment
- Email support
- 5% royalty on revenue above $100K

### Business License ($2,499/year)
- Up to $500K annual revenue
- 3 production deployments
- Priority email support
- 3% royalty on revenue above $500K

### Enterprise License ($9,999/year)
- Unlimited revenue and deployments
- Phone support
- Custom integrations
- Source code access
- 1% royalty on all revenue
